See Tamela Mann's Daughter Tia's Sweet Tribute on Mom's 54th Birthday

David and Tamela Mann's youngest daughter, Tia shared lovely images of her mother and described her as her "angel on earth" in a sweet birthday tribute.

Tamela Mann received a sweet birthday tribute from her daughter, Tia as she turned 54. On her Instagram, David Mann and Tamela’s youngest shared images of her mother and offered sweet words to match.

Tamela Mann at the Stellar Gospel Music Awards in March 2018 in Las Vegas.| Photo: Getty Images

“Y’all help me wish this beautiful [butterly emoji] a Happy birthday,” Tia wrote to caption her slide show. She added, “Love you mommy!! My angel on earth.”

Tia, who fondly calls herself “the baby of the Mann clan” is one of two children David and Tamela share together. Apart from her older brother, David Jr., the siblings also have two half-sisters, Porcia and Tiffany, from David’s relationships outside of his marriage to Tamela. Tia also has a cousin, Sonya, who Tamela raised as her own after her sister's death.

Fans of the Manns got a closer look at Tia through her family’s show, “It’s a Mann’s World” and more recently, “The Manns.” We know her as a singer and the spoiled member of the family.

Tia’s struggle with her weight has been highlighted on the show.

In a clip for their show, her father described her as a combination of him and Tamela. He also said she’s “compassionate to a fault.” Tamela agreed and added that her daughter is “so giving.”

Tia’s struggle with her weight has been highlighted on the show. In 2017, the year her mother flaunted her astonishing weight loss, Tia worried over a liquid diet she was supposed to get into because she didn’t want to give up the other food she enjoyed. It was a dilemma her father tried to help her with by encouraging her to stick with the diet.

A year later, Tia flaunted her curves after successfully shedding pounds and she looked magnificent. “Bringing sexy back,” she captioned a photo where she’s dressed for a wedding in a figure-hugging black gown with a thigh-high slit.

To this day, Tia has managed to keep her curves. She flaunted them recently when she marked her 30th birthday with a photo on her Instagram.

Meanwhile, David and Tamela had best wishes for her as they commemorated the milestone in a post flaunting their daughter’s beauty. The proud parents expressed their disbelief that their baby was thirty but looked forward to what the future held for her.
