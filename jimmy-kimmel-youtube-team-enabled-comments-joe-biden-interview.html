Fans Were Stunned That Jimmy Kimmel's YouTube Team Enabled Comments Following His Disastrous Joe Bid

Highlights

  • Jimmy Kimmel's interview with President Joe Biden received negative feedback from fans, who felt Biden was underprepared.
  • Kimmel's YouTube team deactivated comments for Stormy Daniels' interview, but left them open for Biden's, leading to critical comments.
  • Jimmy Kimmel refused to tone down his content about Donald Trump, even when warned by ABC, potentially losing some fans.

Fans have noticed certain trends on Jimmy Kimmel'sYouTube channel. If a topic is too controversial, his team might shut off comments completely. However, that wasn't the case for his interview with President Joe Biden, and fans felt like that was a major mistake. The general reception looking back was that Biden was underprepared, and that Kimmel saved the President multiple times during the interview two years ago.

In the following, we're going to take a look back at the conversation, and what fans had to say. It wasn't as bad as the recent debate, but in the eyes of many, very underwhelming.

We're going to reveal what was discussed, and other interviews Kimmel's team shut comments off for. We will also touch on Kimmel's history with Donald Trump.

Joe Biden's Interview With Jimmy Kimmel Resembled His Recent Presidential Debate, And Fans Let Him Have It

Back in 2022, it was a special episode of Jimmy Kimmel Live, with President Joe Biden as the guest. Heading into the conversation, Biden was already receiving heat for his lack of appearances on talk shows during his run as President, which was unlike past Presidents such as Barack Obama, and even Donald Trump.

Of course, the conversation was politically motivated, and touched on several different issues in the US at the time. A couple of years later, most are still worth discussing.

A summary of Biden and Kimmel's interview:

  • School shooting in America,
  • Passing an Executive Order.
  • Strides in climate change.
  • Joe Manchin & Kyrsten Sinema,
  • Rising food and gas prices.
  • Negative impact of the pandemic.
  • Mental Health Care.
  • Changes in the press.
  • Hope for the US.

The lengthy interview certainly was diverse. However, the outcome was not a positive one. Generally, fans felt as though the President was all over the place throughout the interview. In addition, the majority of viewers could not believe that Kimmel's YouTube team left comments open. A near 48K wrote in, and the most liked comments were anything but positive.

What were the most liked comments saying?

  • "Heartwarming to read the comments and see that the public is not buying the BS."
  • "Props for having the guts to leave the comments section enabled. I knew right away it was gonna be wild lol."
  • "I started watching this interview as a Democrat. I’m now a Republican. Thanks, Jimmy."
  • "Can’t describe how glad I am that I looked in the comments and everyone was thinking the same thing. Y’all restored my faith in this country a bit more."

Jimmy Kimmel's YouTube Team Did Turn Off Comments Following Stormy Daniels' Interview

Close

Perhaps the most controversial interview in the history of Jimmy Kimmel Live, Stormy Daniels appeared on the show promoting her book, Full Disclosure. Kimmel looked extra bubbly throughout the interview as the adult star detailed her time with Donald Trump. “I’m pretty sure I thought he was channeling Hugh Hefner,” said Daniels upon entering Trump's hotel room. "Significantly less sexy.”

Unlike her first time on the show, Daniels was very open this time around. She discussed what Trump was watching in his hotel room (Shark week), along with the items that could be found in the former President's bathroom.

Jimmy Kimmel was having a blast throughout the chat. However, the fan reaction was limited as Jimmy Kimmel's YouTube team wisely turned off the comments. In all likelihood, that was done to protect Stormy Daniels from the backlash. Let's face it, Stormy appearing on the show alone took a lot of courage, especially knowing how controversial it was going to be.

Jimmy Kimmel Was Asked To Tone Down The Donald Trump Content, But Refused

Jimmy Kimmel's feud with Donald Trump would almost lead to the host leaving his position after several years on late night. Kimmels admits ABC warned him about the Trump content, especially during Donald's run as President. Still, Kimmel wouldn't oblige, and was willing to step down if that was going to be the plan of action.

  • “There was at one time, maybe, I don’t know, right around the beginning of this whole Trump thing where … that was kind of hinted at, but I just said, ‘Listen, I get it. I don’t disagree. I mean, you’re right."
  • "I just said, ‘If that’s what you want to do, I understand and I don’t begrudge you for it, but I’m not going to do that. So if you want somebody else to host the show, then that’s fine. That’s OK with me. I’m just not going to do it like that’ … I have lost half of my fans — maybe more than that."

Kimmel went on to admit that he was at one point the most popular late night host among Republicans.

“Ten years ago, among Republicans, I was the most popular talk show host, at least according to the research that they did.”

It'll certainly make for some interesting months of television. We can expect more shots to be fired in the upcoming days, weeks, and months.
