Jeremy Renner Biography, Height, Weight, And Everything You Need To Know

Jeremy Renner is an American actor, musician, and songwriter. He was born on January 7, 1971, in Modesto, California, United States. Renner gained widespread recognition for his role as Clint Barton/Hawkeye in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) films. Here’s everything you need to know about him:

Jeremy Renner Biography

Jeremy Renner grew up in a working-class family in Modesto. He developed an interest in acting and performing at a young age and participated in various school plays and theater productions. Renner attended Modesto Junior College before transferring to the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), where he studied computer science and criminology.

Renner’s acting career began in the late 1990s, with appearances in television shows like “Angel” and “The Net.” He gained critical acclaim for his role as Jeffrey Dahmer in the 2002 film “Dahmer.” Renner’s breakthrough came in 2008 when he portrayed Sergeant First Class William James in the war drama “The Hurt Locker,” which earned him an Academy Award nomination for Best Actor.

Jeremy Renner Height and Weight

Jeremy Renner stands at approximately 5 feet 10 inches (178 cm) tall, and his weight is around 170 pounds (77 kg).

Jeremy Renner Notable Roles

One of Renner’s most notable roles is that of Clint Barton/Hawkeye in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He first appeared as Hawkeye in the 2011 film “Thor” and has since reprised the role in multiple films, including “The Avengers” (2012), “Avengers: Age of Ultron” (2015), “Avengers: Infinity War” (2018), and “Avengers: Endgame” (2019).

Renner has also appeared in other notable films such as “The Town” (2010), “Mission: Impossible – Ghost Protocol” (2011), “American Hustle” (2013), and “Arrival” (2016). He played the lead role of Aaron Cross in “The Bourne Legacy” (2012) and starred as Mayor Carmine Polito in “American Hustle,” earning an Academy Award nomination for Best Supporting Actor.

Jeremy Renner Music Career

In addition to acting, Jeremy Renner is also a musician and songwriter. He released his debut studio album, “The Medicine,” in 2020. The album showcases his singing and songwriting abilities and features a blend of rock, country, and folk music.

Jeremy Renner Personal Life

Renner has a daughter named Ava Berlin Renner, born in 2013. He is known for his privacy and prefers to keep his personal life away from the public eye.

Jeremy Renner has established himself as a versatile actor with a wide range of roles. He continues to be a prominent figure in the entertainment industry, captivating audiences with his performances on both the big screen and in the music world.
