Attitudes Of Juan Rivera Regarding His Wife

The surname of singer and actor Juan Rivera is well-known. In the Mexican music industry, the Rivera family is well-known for its singers and businesspeople such as Juan, Jenni Rivera, Lupillo Rivera, Rosie Rivera, and Chiquis Rivera.

Jenni was one of them, and she achieved tremendous success in music and other ventures. In addition to being a successful entrepreneur, television producer, ambassador, and philanthropist, she is also a television producer.

Several publications, including Billboard and The New York Times, have labeled her the best-selling female singer in regional Mexican music and the most important woman. On December 9, 2012, the renowned artist perished in an aircraft crash in Mexico.

Even after her untimely demise, Jenni’s contributions to Mexican music are lovingly remembered. Unlike her brother, Juan, Jenni has maintained the Rivera family name alive and well. He was arrested on multiple occasions and spent twenty years in prison.

The Scandals of Juan Rivera and Brenda

His Wife Juan was arrested for drug possession in February 1995, when he was 17 years old. Due to his horrible prison experience, he turned suicidal.

Among the abuses he witnessed was the dehumanization of inmates. For instance, in order to humiliate and degrade the prisoners, the cooks urinated and spit on the meals.

Due to the difficulty of surviving in such a hostile atmosphere, the singer considered suicide. Yet, he gathered himself and made the best of his situation.

A few years after his release from prison, he was imprisoned once more. This time, he was sentenced to twenty years in prison for illegally possessing firearms.

Juan suffered from alcoholism due to the tragedies and disarray in his life. He started drinking at age 15, and by age 30, he knew he had developed an addiction.

However, Brenda, the singer’s wife, was always by his side while he was in and out of prison. Juan calls his wife “the one who transformed him.”

Brenda met the artist when she was 17 years old, and she has improved his life. Brenda became pregnant with their first child just six months and a few days after the couple began dating.

The first several years of their relationship were tough, but they did not give up. Instead, they dealt with the situation, and Juan altered himself to become a more suitable spouse for Brenda.

After more than two decades of dating, the couple got married in 2017. Only close friends and family members were present at the beautiful ceremony.

The couple wed during the golden hour, with tearful eyes and deep vows, while the singer’s older brother, Pedro, served as the pastor. The couple and their four children currently reside in California. Juan is concentrating on his music career and aims to carry on the memory of his sister.
