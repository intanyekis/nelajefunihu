DJ Paul Net Worth

Wiki Biography

Paul Beauregard was born on the 13th October 1977, in Memphis, Tennessee USA. DJ Paul is a hip hop artist, songwriter, DJ and record producer, well known for his aforementioned solo career, as well as being the founding member of the band Three 6 Mafia. More, DJ Paul added finances to the overall size of his net worth as a successful entrepreneur. He has been seen in the entertainment industry since 1988.

Has DJ Paul accumulated a lot over his long spanned career? Reportedly, the total size of DJ Paul’s net worth currently stands at an estimated $15 million.

DJ Paul Net Worth $15 Million

At first, DJ Paul was focused on the career of a DJ more than a hip hop artist, but debuting in 1988 he managed to release several solo tapes. In collaboration with his half brother, Lord Infamous, three albums were released under the act of DJ duo The Serial Killaz. In 1991, DJ Paul together with Lord Infamous and Juicy J founded the hip hop band Three 6 Mafia. Later, Gangsta Boo, Koopsta Knicca and Crunchy Black joined the band. It is worth mentioning the fact that the band members founded the record label Hypnotize Minds in 1994, which has also added to the net worth of PJ Paul and other members of the Three 6 Mafia band.

Together with the band, DJ Paul has released 40 singles, 10 studio albums, six compilation albums, five collaboration albums, six mixtapes, EPs and an independent album. Among the most successful singles are those which received certifications for sales including “Stay Fly” (2005), “Poppin’ My Collar” (2006), “Doe Boy Fresh” (2007) and “Lolli Lolli (Pop That Body)” (2008). However, one of their songs was exceptional – worth the Academy Award as the Best Original Song – “It’s Hard out Here for a Pimp” (2005) written by DJ Paul which later was enlisted in the album “Hustle & Flow: Music From and Inspired by the Motion Picture” (2005) the soundtrack album for the feature film “Hustle & Flow” (2005). The studio albums which were certified platinum or gold were “Chapter 2: World Domination” (1997), “When the Smoke Clears: Sixty 6, Sixty 1” (2000), “Da Unbreakables” (2003) and “Most Known Unknown” (2005). It should be noted that as the best group Three 6 Mafia was nominated many times though won the American Music Awards in the previously mentioned category only once.

From time to time DJ Paul has appeared on the stage as a solo artist. His discography includes eight singles, three studio albums and six mixtapes. His debut album “Underground Volume 16: For Da Summa” (2002) is certified gold and reached 24th position on the Billboard R&B Top 100 in USA.

Also, as a popular personality he is often invited into various television programs, such as the reality television show “Adventures in Hollyhood” (2007), cooking show “Famous Food” (2011) in which he won the first prize, and others. All the appearances on television and cinema have increased not only the popularity of the DJ Paul but also his net worth.

Concerning other business ventures, Three 6 Mafia released films under the franchise “Choices”. DJ Paul is the founder of the record label Scale-A-Ton Entertainment and a real estate agency. Together with Juicy J he launched the clothing line “Dangerus/Skandulus”. DJ Paul started his BBQ seasonings “DJ Paul BBQ”. All these ventures also have added significantly to the net worth of DJ Paul.

Finally, in the personal life of DJ Paul, he is engaged to his long time girlfriend Majda Baltic.
