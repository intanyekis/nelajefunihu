Quinton Perry of Brookville passes away in car accident in Jefferson County

The Brookville community in Pennsylvania is grappling with profound sadness following the tragic passing of Quinton Perry, who lost his life in a motor vehicle accident in Jefferson County. The accident resulted in severe injuries for Quinton, ultimately leading to his untimely death.

Kayleigh Rhodes of Brookville, Pennsylvania, expressed her deep sorrow on Facebook, extending thoughts and prayers to Quinton’s friends, family, and all others involved in the accident. Despite knowing Quinton for a short time during their participation in track, she remembered him as a kind and caring individual who had a talent for lifting the spirits of those around him with his sense of humor. Quinton’s ability to bring laughter to those in his presence left a lasting impact, and his absence will be keenly felt by many who knew him.

DuBois Central Catholic, recognizing the profound impact Quinton had on the community, will have a blue day this week to show support, celebrate his life, and honor his memory. This gesture serves as a testament to the unity and shared grief of the community in the wake of this tragic event.

As the community mourns the loss of Quinton Perry, friends, family, and well-wishers come together to remember the joy and laughter he brought into their lives. His untimely departure is a somber reminder of the fragility of life, and the collective support of the community stands as a tribute to the indelible mark Quinton left on those who had the privilege of knowing him. May he rest in peace.
