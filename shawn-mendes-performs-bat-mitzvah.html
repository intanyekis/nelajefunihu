Shawn Mendes Performs At Big Money Bat Mitzvah

A Bat Mitzvah to remember! Singer and heartthrob Shawn Mendes performed at his very first bat mitzvah this past week and he couldn’t have been more excited.

The singer, while performing his hit song “Mercy” told the crowd of 275 13-year-olds, yes, you read that number right, that “this is my first bat mitzvah actually, and I’m very excited to be here".

Although the crowd was smaller than his normal thousand seat stadium concerts, the paycheck certainly was not! Shawn Mendes performed at Ella Bikoff’s bat mitzvah, who is the daughter of J. Darius Bikoff, founder and creator of Vitaminwater and Smartwater.

According to TMZ, the party itself cost Bikoff an estimated $5 to 7 million, which isn’t much for the multi-millionaire. This wasn’t the first time Bikoff spent an astonishing amount on a private concert; he surprised his wife for their 10th anniversary with a private Justin Timberlake performance costing him $1.3 million.

Although Shawn was the highlight of the night, the party didn’t stop there in regards to over the top surprises. Bikoff also had 40 to 50 horse carriages waiting to escort the guests from the synagogue to the party, which wasn’t at your run of the mill dancehall, but the extra fancy Plaza Hotel in New York City.

We’re sure this is a party that daughter Ella will never forget, and a performance we're certain Mendes will be happy to deposit!
