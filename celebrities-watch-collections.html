10 Celebrities Who Have Huge Watch Collections

Lots of people collect different kinds of things. Some of those things are cheap, as well as easy to find, while others are expensive and rare, which adds to their value. One of the things a lot of individuals enjoy collecting is watches.

There are many cool watches that every collector should definitely try to get their hands on. One of the nicest ones that should be in everyone’s closet if they enjoy wearing a watch is the Rolex Submariner. This watch is classy and chic, but it is also very tough, which makes it one that everybody should own if they’re able to afford it.

Since watches are so fashionable, there are lots of celebrities who like to collect them. Here are some famous people that have watch collections.

Jay-Z

Rapper Jay-Z has been spotted wearing a lot of beautiful watches, which means that he must be really into collecting them. The rapper has even named one of the watches he loves in a song of his.

In the song “Paris,” the talented rapper talks about the Audemars Piguet Royal Oak Offshore watch. Back in the early 2000s, Audemars Piguet even released a watch in his honor, which represents just how close of a relationship he has with them.

But that is not the only watch the talented performer has. He has also been seen wearing Richard Mille RM027 watch as well.

Bradley Cooper

Bradley Cooper is a very busy guy these days, but he is not too busy to have an impressive and awesome collection of watches. The actor and director has been spotted wearing a number of really nice watches, including the IWC Big Pilot.

Bradley has actually worn that watch so much that he became a brand ambassador for their company a long time ago. The filmmaker has even worn this type of watch in some of his movies.

He also owns a vintage watch as well. The star owns a Rolex GMT-Master Rootbeer, which is something he supposedly got from Clint Eastwood, whom he worked with when he filmed the movie American Sniper.

Robert Downey Jr.

Anyone who has paid attention to actor Robert Downey Jr. knows that he is a serious fan of watches. He has been seen wearing numerous really nice ones over the years.

In one of his Instagram posts, some of his fans noticed that he was wearing an Omega Speedmaster, but something about it was not quite right. Those who gave it a really close look noted that there was something really weird about the dial.

But that does not make the watch any less special. He also owns a Bell & Ross watch that he received from a co-star after they finished filming Tropic Thunder, as well as some other really nice watches.

Kanye West

It seems that Kanye Wests’ left wrist is always decorated with something pretty since he has a very nice collection of watches. One of the watches that the famous rapper has been seen wearing a time or two is the Rolex President Day-Date watch, which is usually something a person wears after they have accomplished many great things.

The star also has a very unique version of the Rolex Submariner. Kanye wanted something that was different than what all the other stars are wearing, so he altered the appearance of this watch to make it a bit more unique and special.

Charlie Sheen

Charlie Sheen is one of many famous stars that enjoys collecting watches, and he has some seriously sought-after pieces. The former Two And A Half Men star has been known to own some great watches that are not always easy for everyone to find (or afford).

Back in 2010, the star was in the news because of the fact that one of his watches went missing. The watch in question was a Patek Phillippe 5970, which is was very popular amongst the rich and famous at the time. Just a few hours before it went missing, he was having dinner with someone while wearing it.

Leonardo DiCaprio

Leonardo DiCaprio is another celebrity that likes to collect watches. While many of them like to own Rolex watches, he actually only owns one of those, and that is the Rolex Cosmograph Daytona.

The famous actor also owns a Tag Heuer Aquaracer. Leonardo actually has a partnership with the company that makes this watch, and he has been one of their ambassadors since 2009. Part of what makes this watch so nice and luxurious is the fact that it can go under a certain amount of water without being seriously damaged. This watch is a must-have for anyone who collects watches.

Usher

Usher is really into collecting cool and expensive watches, much like some of his celebrity peers. One of the watches he owns is a Rolex Cosmograph Daytona. This watch has a very bright shade of gold on the case, which makes it a very visually attractive watch to have.

The star also owns the Cartier Ballon Bleu, which has a very unique appearance. It is very easy to see why he loves this watch so much.

Another watch that is a part of Usher’s collection is the Breitling Navitimer, This watch is one that lots of pilots seem to really appreciate.

Ellen DeGeneres

Anyone who watches The Ellen DeGeneres Show knows that the host is a very big fan of watches. She can almost always be spotted wearing a different one than she was wearing the day before.

One of the watches her fans have spotted on her before is the Rolex Day-Date II 228238, which has a different case than some of the others that are like it. Ellen clearly loves to have modern watches, but she also won’t turn down some older watches, either. The television show host owns a GMT-Master 1675, which is an incredibly rare watch that not everyone can get their hands on.

Joel McHale

Joel McHale is one of the most well-known celebrity watch collectors there is. He is very open about his love of watches, and it seems as though the star is always up for having a fun conversation about timepieces.

Joel is such a big collector of watches that he actually has a really solid relationship with Glashütte Original. For those who do not know, that is a company that specializes in creating luxurious watches. There are lots of pictures of Joel wearing beautiful watches, so anyone who has seen the star probably has not had a very hard time figuring out that he is a well-known watch enthusiast.

Sylvester Stallone

Sylvester Stalone is most known for his iconic roles in the films he has been a part of, but the actor is also known for his love of watches since he has a very impressive collection of them. Apparently, the actor is one of many famous faces that can be seen with a Rolex Submariner. He has actually been wearing that kind of watch since he was starring in the Rocky film series, so it seems that he is relatively loyal to that company.

Sylvester also owns a Rolex Deepsea Seadweller, It seems as though he might actually own two of them. The actor showed one of those watches off on social media.
