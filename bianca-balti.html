Bianca Balti is an Italian model who has done modeling work for a variety of brands and designers such as Roberto Cavalli, Donna Karan, Christian Dior, Gucci, Fendi, D&G, Valentino, Paco Rabanne, Anna Molinari, Guerlain, Revlon, Armani Jeans, Missoni, Rolex, Guess, Mango, and Thierry Mugler. She has also been featured on the cover of popular magazines such as Vogue, W, Cosmopolitan, Harper’s Bazaar, Marie Claire, and many more. Bianca has been the face of brands like Cesare Paciotti, Dolce & Gabbana, and many more. She was also seen in the 2008 film, Go Go Tales.
