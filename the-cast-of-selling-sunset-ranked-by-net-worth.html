The Cast Of Selling Sunset, Ranked By Net Worth

  • Jason And Brett Oppenheim ($50 million Each)

Adam DiVello created Selling Sunset for Netflix, which premiered in 2019. The show revolves around the story of a high-end real estate brokerage firm named Oppenheim Group where the stars navigate their personal and professional life through every passing episode. Since 2019, the show has released four seasons and gained immense popularity for its professional yet personal take on the lives of well-known agents working at a popular firm. By selling some of the most luxurious homes in Los Angeles, the cast of the Oppenheim group has made millions through their careers and by starring on the show.

With a stunning and glamorous cast that keeps trending on the internet, the popularity of the show has grown exponentially in the last few years. While changes are going on in their personal lives, these realtors have the best expertise to keep the business growing and make millions through sales.

Amanza Smith ($1 million)

Amongst the elite working at the Oppenheim Group, Amanza Smith is one of the most dedicated cast members. She is a model and a businesswoman who strives to sell properties while overcoming problems in her personal life. Smith’s consistent work, the popularity of the show and her design business called AmanZa LLP ensured her a $1 million fortune as stated by The Things

Maya Vander ($1 million)

With a bulk of her earnings coming from real estate, Maya Vander has accumulated a $1 million net worth working with the Oppenheim Group and the Douglas Elliman agency. Working in the Los Angeles market, Vander has maintained good relationships with both companies that have given her career a boost and she is popularly titled as the multi-tasker on Selling Sunset.

Mary Fitzgerald ($1 million)

A former girlfriend of Jason Oppenheim and now married to Romain Bonnet, Mary Fitzgerald is a hot topic on the internet due to her history with her boss. The broker’s ability to sell luxurious homes on Selling Sunset has earned her popularity around the world, as reported by Capital FM, the star realtor has managed to rake a $1 million net worth over the years.

Christine Quinn ($1.5 million)

Viewers are interested to know more about the drama that centers around Christine Quinn, the glamorous star of Selling Sunset. The actress has been working with Oppenheim Group for more than five years and has garnered a $1.5 million fortune while working with the company. A Dallas-native, Quinn moved to California to pursue a modeling and acting career but found her calling as a realtor.

Davina Potratz ($2 million)

The first season saw Davina Potratz as a recurring star however she was promoted to a series Regular in the second season. An expert agent, she is a member of the elite and prestigious Top Agent Network. According to Scmp, Potratz was previously seen starring in Million Dollar Listing on Bravo. Her realtor firm and position in the agent network have helped her amass a $2 million net worth.

Heather Rae Young ($3 million)

A woman of many talents, Heather Rae Young has worked in several fields before joining Selling Sunset. The star has worked as a pilates instructor, Playboy model, and made minor appearances in movies and television shows that have raked her net worth to $3 million. Heather got married to a fellow reality star in a highly-anticipated ceremony during the fourth season of the show.

Chrishell Stause ($5 million)

One of the most talked-about stars on Selling Sunset, Chrishell Stause is not afraid to share her personal life and struggles on the show. The realtor is also the center of much gossip as she has previously dated Jason Oppenheim. The star has previously worked in popular soap opera television shows like All My Children and Days Of Our Lives and has amassed a $5 million net worth, as reported by The Sun.

Jason And Brett Oppenheim ($50 million Each)

Bosses, mentors, and love interests, Jason and Brett Oppenheim are the main pillars of the show. They are the owners of the Oppenheim Group where the star realtors sell various properties on Selling Sunset. The brothers are ranked amongst the best real estate agents in America and are the richest on the list. The company was established by their ancestors and now the Oppenheims strive for gaining more success. They have a net worth of $50 million each and collectively of $100 million.

Offering stunning homes and drama, Selling Sunset has become one of the most popular real estate reality shows on Netflix. The fifth season of the show has already been filmed and is awaiting a 2022 release date.
