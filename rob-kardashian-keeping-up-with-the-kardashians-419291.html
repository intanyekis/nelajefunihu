Rob Kardashian Unveils Engagement To Blac Chyna On 'KUWTK' Season 12

One of reality television’s longstanding shows is poised for another comeback this spring. Ahead of Sunday night’s (May 1) premiere of Keeping Up with the Kardashians‘ twelfth season, viewers will see how some of the family’s controversial events took place, specifically Rob Kardashian‘s engagement to Blac Chyna.

Within the super trailer, the lone brother shared the exciting moment about proposing to his now fiancee, but Kourtney, Kim and Khloe seemed less than enthusiastic about the news.

“I don’t want no fighting,” he’s heard saying on the phone, to which Kim replies, “You make sure that your girlfriend doesn’t publicly bash your sisters.” The next scene shows that Kourtney and Khloe both learned of their brother’s headlining news on social media, a proposal that Rob did in front of Blac Chyna’s entire family.

“Listen to the hostility in your voice. That’s exactly why I don’t say anything,” Rob said. “This is the first time I ever proposed to someone in my life. Don’t come at me with some bullsh*t.”

Other instances that’ll play out on screen include Lamar Odom’s major appearance at Kanye West’s Madison Square Garden fashion show, Tyga and Kylie Jenner’s relationship, and much more. Check out the trailer below ahead of the 9 p.m. show on E! tonight.
