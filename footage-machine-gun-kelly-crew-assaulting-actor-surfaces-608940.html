MGK's Crew Assaults Actor, Footage Surfaces

Machine Gun Kelly‘s crew might be in hot water after a video confirms that an alleged assault against Gabriel “G-Rod” Rodriguez is true.

The Sept. 14 incident occurred in Atlanta Ga and according to TMZ, the altercation started when G-Rod called out the “Rap Devil” rapper for his beef with Eminem.

MGK and his people were seated in a restaurant when the actor walked up to him, recording on his phone. The surveillance video from the hotel shows a member of the 28-year-old’s entourage run and pick the actor up from behind and slam him onto the ground. Moments later three other men join and stomp Rodriguez mercilessly.

Two of the men have been identified as MGK’s drummer, ROOK and another who goes by the name fo Slim.

When asked about his injuries Gabriel Rodriguez told TMZ he was badly injured and would only drop the charges against Kelly if he met him in Octagon for a fight. Rodriguez even offered to allow MGK the opportunity to fight alongside one of his friends to meet his weight lass.

“Real f**king manly of this b**ch!!! Tell him I’ll take him plus one of his goons to get him to my weight class!!! And I’ll drop all charges!!” MGP***Y,” said the Power guest star.
